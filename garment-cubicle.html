Garment Cubicle

Dynamic Garment Cubicle & Shoe rack cabinet made of SS 304 to store at least 10 garments along with ten set of shoes, disposable head gear and face mask. The Garment Cubicle is with Blower, HEPA filter and UV Lamp .Blower assembly is with volume control damper for air circulation within the cabinet.

Blower unit is activated upon closing the unit door. The Filtered air re-circulating within the unit removes any floating particle from the suit stored within the unit.
